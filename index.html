lattes

tips

  • chill filter kaapi before using for iced beverages!
  • freeze milk in ice cubes so it won't dilute your drink as it melts
  • do not use boiling water for matcha: bring water to a boil and wait 5 minutes before adding
  • mix syrup with milk and coffee before adding ice
  • avoid almond milk or other strongly flavored milks
  • froth together milk and coffee for a creamy beverage
  • add additional flavor to lattes by frothing spices into milk
  • froth powdered mix-ins such as chocolate powder into hot water or milk before adding ice
  • recycle jam or pasta sauce jars for aesthetic glassware
  • be careful adding ice to a glass jar!
